It is time to save the date for Beantown Stomp 2024! March may be a long way off, but we are already busy planning a delightful dreamy dance weekend.

Beantown Stomp 2024 will be March 15-17 at 1950 Mass Ave in Cambridge MA.

Registration opens December 1st. Sign up here to be kept in the loop about staff, schedule and registration.

Schedule

We're still sorting out details, but the weekend will include:
  • CONTRA DANCING
  • Saturday morning family dance
  • Sunday waltz session
  • Open band opportunities
  • Dance workshops
  • Maybe something special for St. Patrick's Day?
  • Time to see old friends and make new ones
  • Many beautiful beans, including you!
